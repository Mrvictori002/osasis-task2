Personal Career:

Virat Kohli was born on 5 November 1988 in Delhi into a Punjabi Hindu family. His father, Prem Kohli, worked as a criminal lawyer and his mother, Saroj Kohli, served as a housewife. He has an older brother , Vikas, and an older sister, Bhawna. Kohli's formative years were spent in the Uttam Nagar and commenced his early education at Vishal Bharti Public School. According to his family, Kohli exhibited an early affinity for cricket as a mere three-year-old. He would pick up a cricket bat, display natural skill, and request his father to bowl to him. Kohli's cricketing talent was evident early on, and his family supported his passion for the sport. He received his initial coaching at the West Delhi Cricket Academy, where he honed his skills and developed into a promising young cricketer. His dedication and hard work in his childhood and teenage years laid the foundation for his future success in international cricket.

Virat Kohli got married to Bollywood actress Anushka Sharma on December 11, 2017, in a private ceremony in Tuscany, Italy. Their wedding was a highly anticipated and celebrated event in both the cricket and entertainment industries. Following their marriage, the couple continued to be in the public eye due to their respective careers and high-profile status.It was truly a fairytale wedding that left everyone in awe. The couple tied the knot in December 2017 and welcomed their daughter in January 2021. Virat Kohli and Actress Anushka Sharma’s Daughter,Vamika Kohli was born on 11 January 2021 in Breach Candy Hospital Trust, Mumbai, India.
